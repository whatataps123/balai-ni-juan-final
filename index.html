About Balai ni Juan

A quite, romantic private venue and close-to-nature events place. It is a perfect venue for your memorable moments. A pavilion is sheltered by tall and fruit-bearing trees which give a unique charm and refreshing ambiance. It has a koi pond stairway leading to the entrance of the pavilion.

Ideal for birthdays, garden wedding, debuts christening, anniversaries, reunions, and other special events and occassions.
